Building a Better Tomorrow

NU JOUR is a black-owned company focused on innovation, development, and empowering communities across South Africa.

Who We Are

Founded in 2021 by Owethu Madoa and Nkosinathi Masilela, NU JOUR represents a “New Day”. We operate across multiple sectors including engineering, energy, and development, with a strong focus on community upliftment.
